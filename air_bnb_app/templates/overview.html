Objective

In this project, we will analyze current and predict future Airbnb prices by creating a machine learning model on select categorical data.

We intend our resulting model to be used by both Airbnb hosts and renters for price planning: potential hosts can validate Airbnbâ€™s official price model for accuracy, and potential renters can plan travel according to best value by desired categorical attributes.
