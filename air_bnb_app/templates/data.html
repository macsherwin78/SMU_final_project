Data

We identified Airbnb as a potential data source for ML analysis due to their popularity and current price prediction model. Their national and international popularity provides a large amount of data for ML training, and their current price prediction model- available only to potential hosts- would provide a comparison to our final models.

We downloaded our original dataset from Kaggle’s “New York City Airbnb Open Data”. This file is sourced from Inside Airbnb’s 2019 New York City, NY, US ‘listings.csv’, which in turn is sourced directly from Airbnb. This dataset lists all active listings in 2019, excluding any new listings posted after 12/04/2019.

Inside Airbnb is an independent non-commercial fair use resource that analyzes publicly available Airbnb listings to identify how Airbnb is interrupting local residential housing markets. Data is verified, cleansed, and aggregated by Inside Airbnb.
